One Year Anniversary of Teen's Murder Approaches

By John Doe | Written on March 27, 2021

Tomorrow marks the one-year anniversary that authorities discovered sixteen- year-old Canton teenager Jayden Peters murdered in his home.

Police have not named any suspects, and a representative from the Canton Precinct says the case remains an active homicide investigation. They are asking the public to share any information that might help them bring those responsible to justice and provide closure for the Peters family and the Canton community. His parents send their thanks to all those who have helped over the past year.
